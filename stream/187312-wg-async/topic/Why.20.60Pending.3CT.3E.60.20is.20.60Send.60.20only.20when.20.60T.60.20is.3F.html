Topic: Why `Pending<T>` is `Send` only when `T` is?

Waffle Lapkin (Feb 22 2022 at 19:46):

Currently core::future::Pending<T> is Send only when T is Send. This is due to PhantomData<T> used inside.

But since Pending<T> never actually holds or produces Ts, this restriction is not necessary. Can we relax this?
