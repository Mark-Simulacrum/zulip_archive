Josh Triplett (Jun 07 2020 at 16:04):

Both the internals forum and the users forum have terms of service that state they're licensed under CC-by-nc-sa. This is not at all ideal, as it isn't an Open Source license, and it means people shouldn't be copying anything from forum posts into code/documentation/rfcs/etc. We should, ideally, get this changed to the Rust license: MIT OR Apache-2.0.

Josh Triplett (Jun 07 2020 at 16:05):

We can't retroactively change the license for old posts, but we could state that all new posts after YYYY-MM-DD are under the new license.

Josh Triplett (Jun 07 2020 at 16:06):
