Jane Lusby [she/her] (Nov 30 2021 at 00:22):

I'm working on updating the contributor survey to include some more positively framed questions so we're not only focused on problems as documented in https://github.com/rust-lang/surveys/issues/182 and I'm currently looking for suggestions for options to include with the following question:

"What are the elements of the rust-lang contribution experience that have had the greatest positive impact on your ability to contribute to and/or maintain Rust?"

Jane Lusby [she/her] (Nov 30 2021 at 00:23):

the question is going to be a multiple choice where each choice has a rating of how relevant it has been, followed by a text field for people to write in any additional experiences that aren't covered by the suggested choices

Jane Lusby [she/her] (Nov 30 2021 at 17:57):

so yea to reiterate why I opened this stream: Does anyone have suggestions for what choices we should provide by default with this question? Here are the options I've got currently

  • Mentorship, outreach, or encouragement from other project members
  • Learning materials and documentation (rustc-dev-guide, forge, github readmes, API docs)
  • Contribution sprints
  • Welcoming environment with clear path to getting involved
