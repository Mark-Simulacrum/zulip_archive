Nick Cameron (Oct 24 2021 at 10:25):

I found "What is/are your preferred language(s) for the following forms of technical communication?" a bit of a weird question - it seems kind of too flexible - nobody is going to want to technical docs in Bengali, technical talks in Portuguese, and conversations in Vietnamese or whatever. We are trying to find out if people prefer English or their something else for each, right? But it also seems to be getting the wrong results. Like people might prefer to have a technical talk in their local dialect or language, but would rather have the talk in the national language than English, we would miss these opinions, even if there is a large desire for say Hindi rather than English, but there are relatively small responses for Marathi/Gujarati/Punjabi/etc

Nick Cameron (Oct 24 2021 at 10:26):
