Topic: What is bors up to?

J. Ryan Stinnett (Jan 29 2021 at 11:46):

Is there a way to see what checks bors is actually waiting on before merging something? Clicking on the pending PR from the bors queue just says check-actions: pending, which is not very useful... Should I instead look at the GitHub actions workflows bors has started...?

bjorn3 (Jan 29 2021 at 12:11):

For various reasons the actual CI runs in the rust-lang-ci/rust fork for rust and not rust-lang/rust. When bors is actually testing a PR, it should comment like https://github.com/rust-lang/rust/pull/81419#issuecomment-769711867. You can see the queue of pending PR's at https://bors.rust-lang.org/queue/rust
