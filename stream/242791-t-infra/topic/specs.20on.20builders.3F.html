Eric Huss (Mar 27 2022 at 22:27):

GitHub has vague information at https://docs.github.com/en/actions/using-github-hosted-runners/about-github-hosted-runners. That is mostly relevant for macOS, as we use special runners for linux and windows.

I think ubuntu-xl is 16 CPU with 64GB RAM with a giant 667G partition.
And I think windows-xl is 8 CPU with 32GB RAM 344G partition.

Eric Huss (Mar 27 2022 at 22:59):

Oh, and there is also a self-host arm runner. It looks to be a relatively modest 14 CPU 16GB RAM system. I'm not sure if the exact specs were ever published anywhere.
