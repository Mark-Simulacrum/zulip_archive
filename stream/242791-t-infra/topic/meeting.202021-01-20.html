Pietro Albini (Jan 20 2021 at 15:21):

last week we discussed the general direction of setting up @rust-lang.org email addresses for (a subset of) contributors

Pietro Albini (Jan 20 2021 at 15:21):

and we reached this conclusion at the end of the meeting:

Pietro Albini (Jan 20 2021 at 15:21):

General overview

Each person will have githubusername@rust-lang.org, and they will be able to choose a custom alias on a first-come first-serve basis. In case of naming conflicts the Core Team will make a the final decision.

The mailbox will be disabled after someone leaves the project. The mailbox should only be used for project business, the CoC will adhere to communications sent through it (as you represent the project with that email) and it might be accessed by members of the project with elevated privileges if the need arises.

Level of service provided

We need both the ability to send and receive emails. It's preferred to have a full mailbox, but if the service we end up using doesn't support it a forwarding alias with SMTP credentials could also work.

Vendor

We won't manage our own mail server. Using a service that provides a full suite of services (mail, document collaboration, storage, video calls, SSO) is preferred to reduce the amount of tools we have to manage. Some members of the team have a slight leaning torwards GSuite.
