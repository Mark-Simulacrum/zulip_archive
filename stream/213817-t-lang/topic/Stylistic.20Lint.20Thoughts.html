Mentioning braces made me think of a potential pivot for rustc vs clippy on some of these things:

Do they affect users of your crate?

For example, rustc encouraging a consistent naming for anything that can be pub is probably more important than for things like lets which don't matter outside. (rustdoc showing method patterns is a weird middle ground, though.) Similarly, how you decide to do brace formatting is irrelevant outside the file, so rustc doesn't need to lint on it.

That reasoning does still comply with @pnkfelix's mission to move unused_parens to clippy, FWIW.
