Topic: Minimum size of an MCP?

Lokathor (Jul 12 2020 at 17:11):

How small of a language change does a change get before it becomes too small for an MCP, and we just put in a PR and people checks boxes and then we merge it?

Now, repr(align(ptr)) seems like a very small change, but it affects how an attribute works so T-Lang has to sign off somewhere of course. How small is too small for an MCP?
