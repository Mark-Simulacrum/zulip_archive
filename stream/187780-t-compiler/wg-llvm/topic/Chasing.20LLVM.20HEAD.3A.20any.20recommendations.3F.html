Manish Goregaokar (Aug 24 2021 at 22:40):

highfive basically only supports one at a time and it can be confusing if two people are tagged because each will expect the other. you can do r? @foo cc @bar

Manish Goregaokar (Aug 24 2021 at 22:42):

github supports multiple reviewer requests, but also you want to avoid not having a DRI. the r?+cc option means that the r?d person can tag in a different reviewer if they don't have time, and the cc'd person can step up and perform reviews if they want to
