Topic: why transmute?

Winter (Dec 15 2021 at 14:40):

why was transmute chosen to do transmutations instead of making something like an unsafe cast? why a function instead of leveraging the existing cast keyword?

Joshua Nelson (Dec 15 2021 at 16:52):

as doesn't allow specifying the types, and it's also overloaded to do many different things. People have actually been introducing new APIs to libstd so as can be used less: https://doc.rust-lang.org/std/primitive.pointer.html#method.cast
