Hanna Kruppe (May 05 2020 at 07:34):

There's an absolutely enormous design space for numeric traits (hierarchies), I would even say there is no single best design for it. libstd is a bad place to explore that kind of space since it's constrained by backwards compatibility and generally wants to be small/focused. There's the num-traits crate and some other crates that address this subject and don't have those constraints.
