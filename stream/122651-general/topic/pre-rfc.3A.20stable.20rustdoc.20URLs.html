@Joshua Nelson The more "official" answer is that the tools are the domain of https://www.rust-lang.org/governance/teams/dev-tools and not of lang.

The tools are of course an important part of the ecosystem, but they're foundationally different from the language. For example, there's no stability promise in the generated documentation.
