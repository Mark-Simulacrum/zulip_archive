Topic: Updating a crate in the Rust compiler

John Paul Adrian Glaubitz (Feb 16 2022 at 11:56):

Hello! I would like to update the libc package in Rust and need to determine the checksum of version 0.118 of the crate. However, I don't know how to obtain the checksum as there is no cargo command for it. Can someone help?

Pietro Albini (Feb 16 2022 at 12:06):

the checksum cargo puts in Cargo.lock is fetched from https://github.com/rust-lang/crates.io-index/blob/master/li/bc/libc, field "cksum" of the version you care about

Pietro Albini (Feb 16 2022 at 12:06):

unless you're integrating with external build systems there should be no reason to fetch the checksum manually I think

Pietro Albini (Feb 16 2022 at 12:08):

cargo update -p libc --precise 0.2.118

in the root of your repository should do the trick and update the Cargo.lock
