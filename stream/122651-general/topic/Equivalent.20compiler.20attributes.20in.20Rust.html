Thom Chiovoloni (Aug 18 2021 at 16:51):

Then yeah, #[repr(u16)] is the equivalent. e.g.

#[repr(u16)]
enum Foo {
    Bar,
    Baz,
}

Foo will be guaranteed to use 16 bits/2 bytes
