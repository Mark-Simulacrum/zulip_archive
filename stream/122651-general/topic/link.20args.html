Asaf Fisher (Nov 07 2021 at 00:55):

How do I add the link-args to the cargo toml file?
cargo rustc -- -C link-args=-nostartfiles

Eric Huss (Nov 07 2021 at 01:07):

If you want it to default to always pass that argument for your crate, your best bet is to probably use a build script which can specify the link-args to use. More information can be found at https://doc.rust-lang.org/cargo/reference/build-scripts.html

bjorn3 (Nov 07 2021 at 23:21):

-nostartfiles probably shouldn't be passed by dependencies as it breaks linking in most cases. It should probably be in .cargo/config.toml in your project dir.
