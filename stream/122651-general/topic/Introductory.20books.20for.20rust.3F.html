scottmcm (Jul 20 2021 at 22:57):

I'm not sure how much resources are available in Rust for people completely new to programming. Regardless, I still wouldn't suggest starting with C or C++ first.

I'd love to see a purpose-made course of Rust for people with zero programming background, though.
