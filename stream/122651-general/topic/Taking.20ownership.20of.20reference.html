Conrad Ludgate (Mar 14 2022 at 20:40):

You'll need to change your function arguments to take in &mut Type mutable references in order to update their values

Conrad Ludgate (Mar 14 2022 at 20:41):

Eg:

fn update_param(param: &mut Param) {
    update_pat(&mut param.pat);
}
