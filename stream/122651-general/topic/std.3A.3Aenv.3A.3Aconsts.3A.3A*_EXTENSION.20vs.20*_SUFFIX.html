Topic: std::env::consts::*_EXTENSION vs *_SUFFIX

Ian Chamberlain (Apr 06 2022 at 13:28):

Can anyone explain the difference between std::env::consts::EXE_SUFFIX and std::env::consts::EXE_EXTENSION (and same for DLL_* I guess)?

The docs don't seem very clear and the examples are rather few, and all the definitions I've found in std::sys::os seem to indicate that SUFFIX is always equal to ".{EXTENSION}". Is there a historical reason for these or something?

Eric Huss (Apr 06 2022 at 16:57):

I think it is just historical. It looks like the _SUFFIX versions came first, and then someone added the _EXTENSION ones. The old APIs used to have a method that included the . and that was changed, but the old consts were kept.

Ian Chamberlain (Apr 06 2022 at 17:13):

Ok, I figured it was something like that but wanted to double check. For new targets I suppose the recommendation is just to have both, and make sure they match?

Eric Huss (Apr 06 2022 at 18:30):

Yea, I would keep the consistency.
