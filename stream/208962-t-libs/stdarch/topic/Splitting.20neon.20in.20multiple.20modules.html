Topic: Splitting neon in multiple modules

Luca Barbato (Oct 29 2021 at 12:00):

I tried to dogfood the neon intrinsics and as they are they are quite cumbersome to look up, would be nice to split the instruction in multiple modules to make easier to pick what you want, do you think it could be acceptable to do that?

scottmcm (Oct 30 2021 at 01:12):

I think the intrinsics are supposed to just follow exactly whatever the vendor does in theirs, and then use the vendor's resources to decide what you want rather than the rustc docs. For example, for the x86 ones people will look at https://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/docs/intrinsics-guide/ rather than hunt through https://doc.rust-lang.org/nightly/core/arch/x86_64/

scottmcm (Oct 30 2021 at 01:12):
