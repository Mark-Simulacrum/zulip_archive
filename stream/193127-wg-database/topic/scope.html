weiznich (Mar 29 2019 at 14:19):

Currently the scope of this potential working group is quite broad. I think that this working group is getting productive it is required to focus on fewer things. (At least for the start.) Out of the initial discourse thread I see the following different themes:

  • Providing a foundation for building your own database

  • Connecting with existing databases:
    * Relational databases
    * NoSQL databases

  • Building tools on top of those connections

I think in the beginning we should focus on of those points. My pick would be 2.1 building Connections to existing relational databases.
