Re "PubGrub with nesting".
I have not yet done it at a production level of quality. So take what I'm saying with a big grain of salt.
But A Term (the parts of an Incompatibility) rely on representing a "One of this Set" semantics. So if the library is a port of PubGrub ( not a more complicated algorithm with similar inspiration ) then it needs to be built around mutually exclusive versions. I.E. I am leaning tord modeling multiple nesting's before handing it to PobGrub.
