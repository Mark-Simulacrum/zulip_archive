Topic: removing trait implementors sidebar section

Noah Lev (Nov 28 2021 at 00:57):

Issue #91118 is about the implementors section of trait pages' sidebars always being empty, and PR #91277 fixes this by filling the sidebar with the implementors.

However, I kind of wonder if we should just remove the implementors (and implementations on foreign types) sidebar sections. They're not part of the trait's interface, and for many traits the list will be very long and fill up the sidebar.

It may not even be a bug that the section is empty; perhaps it's intended just to be a link to the beginning of the implementors section in the main body of the page. (I agree that it looks confusing though as it currently stands.)

This would simplify rustdoc's UI and its implementation, as #91277 is actually a fairly large and somewhat complex change.

What do you think?
