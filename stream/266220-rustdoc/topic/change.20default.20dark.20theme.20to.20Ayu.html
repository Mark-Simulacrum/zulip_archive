Noah Lev (Jan 12 2022 at 02:58):

What do people think of changing the default dark theme to Ayu? Personally, I feel it's more readable than the "Dark" theme.

Of course, some people may prefer the Dark theme over Ayu—which would just invert this annoyance—but I'm wondering at least how you all would feel about changing the default.
