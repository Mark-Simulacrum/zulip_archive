jsha (Nov 01 2021 at 19:27):

@Nick Cameron thanks for posting re: the survey. There's something I've been thinking about how best to ask. Something like this:

Does your editor display inlay hints for type annotations (https://rust-analyzer.github.io/manual.html#inlay-hints)? [yes, no, it can but I turned it off]

If yes, do they make you more productive? [strong disagree, weak disagree, neutral, weak agree, strong agree]

My hypothesis is that seeing the type annotations for inferred types tremendously helps beginners feel more confident they understand what's going on; but talking to more experience Rustaceans, I sometimes hear they find type annotations distracting and unnecessary.
