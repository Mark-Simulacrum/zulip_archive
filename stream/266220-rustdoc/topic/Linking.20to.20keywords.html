Topic: Linking to keywords

ruster (Nov 24 2021 at 19:14):

Is it possible to link to keywords in a fashion of like [\`async\`], [\`await\`] etc.? I really think https://doc.rust-lang.org/std/future/index.html could use links to those 2 keywords but I'm not sure it's possible. Is it?

Noah Lev (Nov 24 2021 at 19:16):

It's possible, but you can't use intra-doc links. You instead have to use something like ../../std/keyword.async.html (with the correct number of ..s based on the current module).

Noah Lev (Nov 24 2021 at 19:16):

Although the Future docs should use ../../core/keyword.async.html since Future is defined in core.

Ah, ok, thanks!
