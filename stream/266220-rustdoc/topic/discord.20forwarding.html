apiraino (Feb 18 2021 at 10:22):

my .2 cents on the matter (that nobody asked ^^):

  • forwarding messages from Discord is IMO unpractical, I'm afraid we would receive a non-actionable "read-only" copy, more akin to a notification and only on a selected number of supported receivers. If I have to go back to Discord to reply, I feel like I've lost the advantage.
  • bridging messages between Zulip and Discord (example through a mega bridge) requires a T-infra effort which I'm not sure they have bandwidth for right now
  • side note: by reading around, Discord is becoming more and more hostile and little by little closing their API
  • the Matrix protocol (which I strongly support for some use cases) has a subpar threading model than Zulip, so I agree with Joshua Nelson here

I understand the problem here and I cringe when communities (even relatively small such as rustdoc) are split because proprietary platforms obtusely refuse to interoperate with open protocols.
