item-infos have this little up arrow pointing to the method they related to. I'd like to get rid of it.

This was originally added because when methods are collapsed, it's hard to tell whether an item-info goes with the thing below it or above it. I think we can accomplish that by making the spacing between methods bigger (like 2rem), and the spacing between a method and its item-info smaller (like 0.5rem).
