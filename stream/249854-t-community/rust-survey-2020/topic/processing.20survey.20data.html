Xidorn Quan (Sep 30 2020 at 11:01):

Actually I'd like to recommend this approach to other languages' team as well, as I find it much more productive and less error-prone than translating inside spreadsheet, and you have more flexibility on the process. For example, we create issues to track what questions need to be translated, and collaborators can assign themselves to the issues so we don't conflict. If some team has enough time and energy, they can even introduce a review process.
