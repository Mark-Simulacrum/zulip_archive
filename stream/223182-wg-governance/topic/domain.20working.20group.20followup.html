Val Grimm (May 03 2020 at 13:39):

Hi all, very late (due to some life stuff) here's my proposed draft of a message to the domain wg leads to ask the questions worked out in the last governance meeting. If this message looks ok, I will send it to the relevant list: Dear domain working group leads,

To better understand your experiences with your working group, the governance committee would appreciate your responses to the following five questions:

1) If you had to start your Working Group over from the beginning, what would you change?
2) What are some notable changes that your group has made to Rust? How do you feel about the process it took to get those changes accepted?
3) How do you feel your group benefits from being part of the Rust organisation?
4) Does your experience of forming a Domain Working Group change how you see the mission of Rust?
5)If relevant, how has your experience with Rust’s Domain Working Groups compared to working in other language domain groups?

Best regards,
Val Grimm, on behalf of the Rust Governance Committee
