The initiative is to approach two goals. 1) to build a cohort or group that will work on the rustc dev guide and 2) to provide a vehicle for people new to compiler to contribute to the dev guide and learn more about the compiler internals.

Doc Jones (Aug 10 2021 at 17:27):
