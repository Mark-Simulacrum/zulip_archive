rustc is a very big rust codebase and so it's not surprising that it's difficult to understand in depth. I am new here, but perhaps one strategy is to just focus on one fairly small piece and work outwards - rather than attempting to understand everything.

my only suggestion is: if you're really motivated, don't give up. As suggested, start small (even a one line fix!) and little by little get yourself familiar with the codebase. You will always find people reviewing and helping with your contributions.
