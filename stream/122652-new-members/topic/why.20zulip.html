Joshua Nelson (Mar 02 2021 at 14:22):

Yes, each team decides where it wants to have communications. No, there is no central government - there is the RFCs repo for major changes to the language, but it doesn't govern "small" or internal things like what platform to use. The way each team makes decisions is also up to them - T-compiler mostly uses MCPs or weekly meetings, while T-rustdoc uses FCPs a lot more because we don't have meetings.
