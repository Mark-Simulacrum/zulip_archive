Topic: Do we still want to support `pipelining = false`?

Josh Triplett (Jan 04 2022 at 20:41):

In the course of working on cargo's internals regarding metadata and timing, I noticed that we still support both pipelined and non-pipelined builds. I wondered, given how long pipelining has been the default, would it make sense to remove the non-pipelined build support and constant-fold away all that code, and just no-op the configuration option (perhaps with a warning)?

Josh Triplett (Jan 04 2022 at 20:42):

Do we still get any value out of that code? Is there an important case where people are disabling pipelining?

Alex Crichton (Jan 04 2022 at 21:11):

Alex Crichton (Jan 04 2022 at 21:11):
