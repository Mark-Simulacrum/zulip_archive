It is generally fine to queue perf requests for PRs -- you might want to check queue (https://perf.rust-lang.org/status.html) but due to CI problems and release week it's been pretty empty, so generally not a problem to queue things in.

The other thing to check is that the PR isn't doing anything weird with CI configs or whatever (like trying to leak secrets or so) but this is a very quick check usually, can just scroll through the diff. (This should be done before any bors try).
