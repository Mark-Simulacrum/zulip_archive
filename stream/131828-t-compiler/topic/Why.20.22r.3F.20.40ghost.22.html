DPC (Sep 09 2020 at 10:15):

A reviewer would increase latency of the rollup thus defeating the purpose of a rollup since some of those prs might get merged in the interim

mati865 (Sep 09 2020 at 10:21):

I suspect because this tricks the system into moving the merge forward because a reviewer is needed

As you have noticed rollup consists of already approved PRs and will get r+ shortly after by the person who made it.
Reviewer is not strictly necessary but rust-highfive bot assigns somebody to each new PR. So to avoid unnecessary pinging of the reviewers @ghost is assigned.
