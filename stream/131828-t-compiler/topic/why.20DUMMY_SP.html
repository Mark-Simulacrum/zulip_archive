Why do we have DUMMY_SP instead of explicitly using Option<Span>?

Aaron Hill (Feb 05 2021 at 22:49):

a span can have no location information, but a non-root SyntaxContext

Aaron Hill (Feb 05 2021 at 22:50):

DUMMY_SP can be thought of can an extension of that - no location information and a root SyntaxContext

Aaron Hill (Feb 05 2021 at 22:50):

so using Option<Span> would introduce multiple representations for the same concept
