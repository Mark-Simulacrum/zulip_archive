Topic: Next LLVM meeting on no-alias support

Félix Fischer (Jul 07 2020 at 07:58):

For anyone who might be interested in participating.

The LLVM-dev Mailing List is really taking the Alias Analysis seriously again, and a group has formed that's working on fixing the shortcomings in the current noalias optimization implementation (which is known to be unsound, and that's why Rust doesn't use it even though it is a super good fit for Rust's borrowing and ownership rules).

This work is open to the public and the group that has formed hosts a public reunion every four weeks. The next one will be on July the 14th.

I'm posting this here for if anyone wants to chip in / represent rustc's perspective / just learn from it. I don't think I can really give them much of my own harvest, but I might go to learn from it.

This is the google document they are using to organize (you can see the past meetings' notes down below).

I hope this is useful to some of y'all :heart:
