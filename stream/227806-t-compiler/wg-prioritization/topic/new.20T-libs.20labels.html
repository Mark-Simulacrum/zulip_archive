pnkfelix (Jun 24 2021 at 14:08):

Out of curiousity, what is motivation for renaming libs-impl to T-libs, rather than just having T-libs-api and T-libs-impl ?

Mara (Jun 24 2021 at 14:09):

because there's quite a few things that are neither strictly api or impl. e.g. rust-lang crates, documentation, reviewer guidelines, etc.

Mara (Jun 24 2021 at 14:11):

so we felt it was important there's a general 'T-libs' so things don't fall in the gap between them. originally the idea was a general T-libs and a specific T-libs-impl, but after some more thinking and discussions, we concluded it would work better to do the split with a general T-libs and specific T-libs-api
