Topic: VisibilityKind::Inherited

Andrea Lattuada (Oct 25 2021 at 12:18):

Hi folks: what does rustc_hir::hir::VisibilityKind::Inherited mean on an impl item? (for an impl block referencing no traits)
I'm trying to figure out if a method is marked pub. Do you know where I may be able to find info on this? (I've had a look at the dev guide unsuccessfully)

Matthew Jasper (Oct 25 2021 at 12:48):

Inherited means that there was no explicit pub annotation on the item. For most things (including inhent impl items) this means private, but for trait items and enum variants it means that this has the same visibility as its parent.

Andrea Lattuada (Oct 25 2021 at 12:50):

Thank you! That make sense. I just found out that I kept seeing Inherited when I expected Public because I was looking at the vis for the top level Item::Impl and not the impl items themselves, oops.
