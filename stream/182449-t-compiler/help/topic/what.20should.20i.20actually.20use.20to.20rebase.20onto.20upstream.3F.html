scottmcm (Jan 19 2021 at 23:25):

FWIW, I have these two aliases:

[alias]
    purr = pull --rebase=true --autostash -Xignore-space-change --no-stat
    prum = purr upstream master

and thus just do git prum in my PR branches when needed.
