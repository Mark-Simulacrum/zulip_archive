bjorn3 (Apr 06 2022 at 17:08):

You can use -Clink-arg=-Wl,-rpath,/usr/local/toolchains/foo/lib64 to modify the rpath to a specific value. As for using a custom glibc I think -Clink-arg=--sysroot=/usr/local/toolchains/foo would be what you want.
