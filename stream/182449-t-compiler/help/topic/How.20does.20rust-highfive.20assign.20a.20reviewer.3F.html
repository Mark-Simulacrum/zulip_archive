Topic: How does rust-highfive assign a reviewer?

osa1 (Jan 26 2021 at 08:07):

Should I trust rust-highfive at assigning someone to a PR or should I assign someone myself? I'm not sure if uses some kind of code ownership or anything like that, or just assigning next person in a list of potential reviewers.

davidtwco (Jan 26 2021 at 09:47):

rust-highfive has a list of reviewers depending on what part of the repository you've changed, as I understand it, it isn't fine-grained (like code owners normally are), but more like "any of the compiler". I believe it picks from those reviewers at random. I tend to let highfive pick a reviewer unless there is someone I know to be better suited (e.g. the person who wrote mentoring instructions, or who I know to work on that part of the compiler a lot).

bjorn3 (Jan 26 2021 at 10:44):
