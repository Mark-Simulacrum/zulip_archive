Topic: How can I emit LLVM IR from library/std

Jake Hughes (Jan 29 2021 at 00:19):

Hi, is there a straightforward way to emit the LLVM IR generated from ./x.py build library/std?

nyms (Jan 29 2021 at 01:37):

you can pass stage specific compilation flags, something like: RUSTFLAGS_NOT_BOOTSTRAP="--emit llvm-ir" ./x.py build --stage-you-want-ir-for library/std
