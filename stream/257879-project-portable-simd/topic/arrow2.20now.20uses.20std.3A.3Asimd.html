Topic: arrow2 now uses std::simd

Jorge Leitao (Mar 12 2022 at 23:28):

Hey, just to bring the news that after some back and forth to fix all the performance regressions against packed_simd, we just released arrow2 using std::simd as its backend for simd (on nightly). Thanks a lot everyone for all the help in making this happen. Users of arrow2 and in particular Polars, one of the fastest DataFrame APIs out there, are now leveraging all the work on portable simd :tada: :tada:
