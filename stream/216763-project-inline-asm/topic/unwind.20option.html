Amanieu (Aug 12 2021 at 01:09):

External assembler is actually rather easy to support unwinding from. You just need:
a) on the caller side, mark the function as potentially unwinding.
b) on the asm side, emit CFI directives to tell the assembler to generate DWARF unwinding info.
