@Cameron Steffen @Takayuki Nakata I added the prioritization labels and a sync-blocker label. I would apply those labels like this:

  • P-low: Requires attention (fix/response/evaluation) by a team member but isn't urgent
  • P-medium: Should be addressed by a team member until the next sync
  • P-high: Should be immediately addressed and will require a out-of-cycle sync or a backport. This is for example an ICE in a popular crate, many other crates depend on.
  • P-sync-blocker: An issue that "blocks" a sync. Or rather: before the sync this should be addressed, e.g. by removing a lint again, so it doesn't hit beta/stable. I will scan for those issues before each sync.
