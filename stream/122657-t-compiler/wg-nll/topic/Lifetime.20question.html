Mario Carneiro (Sep 04 2020 at 09:21):

If you just let borrow regions drop out you can get easy bad behavior, e.g.

let mut v = vec![0];
let x = &'a v[0];
(&'b mut v).push(1);
use(x);

We could say that 'a covers lines 2 and 4 and 'b covers line 3 and then they are disjoint
