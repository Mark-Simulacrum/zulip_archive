nikomatsakis (Jun 25 2021 at 08:59):

I've seconded this, but i'd like to hear from folks who find the recordings valuable. I started the recordings and in the past I've been a big advocate: I want lang team discussions to be transparent. On the other hand, I also want them to have access to all the right people, and I've definitely heard from more than one person a preference against recording. In particular I've heard of people reporting bad experiences with 'bullying' (not sure the best word) on the internet as a result of past recordings, and I wouldn't want to be a part of that.

There have definitely been times when the recording has felt like a hindrance. Lang-team discussions sometimes get emotional. Sometimes individuals (both members of the team and not) act in frustrating ways and it's harder to talk about that when everything is recorded. Like it or not, people tend to calibrate what they say based on who is in the room, and it's a bit unfair to then expose those comments to the internet for posterity.

nikomatsakis (Jun 25 2021 at 09:00):
