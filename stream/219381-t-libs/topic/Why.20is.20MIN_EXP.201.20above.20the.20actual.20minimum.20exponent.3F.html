Topic: Why is MIN_EXP 1 above the actual minimum exponent?

As the topic title says, why are the MIN_EXP values for f32 and f64 both 1 more than the actual minimum exponent of the type?

Thom Chiovoloni (Dec 17 2021 at 06:32):

it's a dumb reason. the exp values come from C's stdlib, which assumes it's v * 2**exp for v in 0.5..=1.0, but ieee754 uses v in 1.0..=2.0

Thom Chiovoloni (Dec 17 2021 at 06:32):
