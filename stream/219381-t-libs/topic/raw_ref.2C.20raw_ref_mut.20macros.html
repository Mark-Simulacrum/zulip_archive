Topic: raw_ref, raw_ref_mut macros

nikomatsakis (Jun 01 2020 at 21:13):

Hey libs team, I wanted to call your attention to https://github.com/rust-lang/rust/pull/72279 -- this PR is adding two macros (raw_ref, raw_ref_mut) which would serve as a way to borrow a raw pointer. This is a change from the older proposal which was to have a dedicated syntax of &raw. You can read more about the reasons for this change here, but in today's meeting, we realized that this does then become kind of "libs surface area", so I wanted to make you all aware of the proposal. These macros would be unstable for now but I expect we would want to move towards stability.

Josh Triplett (Jun 01 2020 at 21:26):

The eventual goal would be to have some sort of built-in syntax for this, but depending on the timeline for such syntax, it may make sense to stabilize the built-in-macro form as well and make them equivalent.
