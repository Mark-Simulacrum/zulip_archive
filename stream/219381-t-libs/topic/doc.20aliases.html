Jubilee (Jun 27 2021 at 21:18):

I believe we should consider two documentation alias sets:
Aliases of the form: "linear_interpolation" for "lerp" or vice versa. These are not about other programming languages, these are about literal ways to say the thing differently.
Certain POSIX, Darwin, Linux, and Windows aliases instead of language aliases for OS-generic system APIs where there is an unequivocal match, especially for the cases where we already do so anyways(!)

Josh Triplett (Jun 27 2021 at 22:18):
