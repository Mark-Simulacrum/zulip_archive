Nick Cameron (Jan 10 2022 at 15:14):

Hi all, I would like to propose we refresh the async foundations WG. I see two issues, the easier one to address is that the official composition of the group (https://github.com/rust-lang/team/blob/master/teams/wg-async-foundations.toml) is out of date, or at least does not reflect who is currently active in doing work, coming to meetings, etc.

The more substantial issue, is that I would like to have an easily addressable group of stakeholders (maintainers of async runtimes, users of async doing particularly interesting things, etc.) who won't want to come to meetings or get every notification, but do want to keep abreast of developments and should be easy to pull in to discussion as required (and have made a commitment to contribute to such discussions when asked). I'm not sure whether that should be a separate group, or a mailing list, or part of our WG or what. Thoughts?

tmandry (Jan 11 2022 at 20:23):

Niko and I were discussing criteria for WG membership. Here's what we came up with:

WG members must have active participation in last 6 months, where participation is one of the following:

    * attended triage/sprint meetings
    * opened PRs
    * reviewed PRs
    * mentored people to fix polish issues
    * led an initiative
