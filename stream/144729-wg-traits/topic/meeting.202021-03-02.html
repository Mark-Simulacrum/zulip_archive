Matthew Jasper (Mar 03 2021 at 18:42):

So, after some experimenting, streaming iterator combinators are not usable (although they can be implemented) due to lack of normalization. There are also some issues with specific combinators:

  • filter and filter_map can't be implemented without unsafe or -Zpolonius
  • filter_map also requires an helper trait because there's no way to write for<'a> FnMut(Self::Item<'a>) -> Option<T<'a>> where T is a hkt
  • flat_map would have to return a self-borrowing struct.

Matthew Jasper (Mar 04 2021 at 16:46):

and:

  • chain requires one of the iterators to be 'static.
