In short: We need to move things into rustc_type_ir. A lot of types impl Encodable/Decodable, some through the TyEncodable/TyDecodable derive. There are two problems here: 1) Encodable/Decodable needs to be moved to or upstream of rustc_type_ir 2) TyEncodable/TyDecodable needs TyCtxt which is in rustc_middle, so we need to figure out a way around the coherence here. (It might mean defining an "Interner" trait that we split out of TyCtxt

Jack Huey (Jan 24 2021 at 17:23):

I imagine in some cases we could just impl from rustc_middle on types from rustc_type_ir, but doesn't work with private fields and such.
