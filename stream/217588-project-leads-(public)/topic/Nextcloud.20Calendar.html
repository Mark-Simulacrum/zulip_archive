XAMPPRocky (Mar 06 2020 at 14:20):

On the topic of the calendar itself. An important feature to me in nextcloud that I haven't been able to find any other calendar service is having a public link containing multiple calendars. The link is just combination of all the calendars shown on the left sidebar. Which means that you can have different calendars with different people and permissions adding events while having a single place that people can see everything.

This is also embeddable, so it would actually be possible to have the team schedule for a given week or month on the website or forge directly. People could easily see what meetings are happening.
