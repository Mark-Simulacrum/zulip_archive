Topic: Web Interface: Customization

Gerald E. Butler (Aug 27 2020 at 17:55):

What options exist for customizing the web interface of Zulip?

In particular, is it possible to have the Users list always be collapsed and expand or open on demand through either menu, keyboard combo, or hove? Also, can the Streams list be widened either automatically based on content or manually by drag? Neither of these seems to be supported, but I want to make sure I haven't missed something.

Cyphase (Aug 27 2020 at 19:19):

Neither of them is supported. The Users list will be hidden in narrow windows, unless you turn on the option that will show it in the left sidebar. Also, in case it's helpful, there is an option to make the app use the full width of the browser.

I would encourage you to open an issue with your thoughts; the Zulip team is very responsive.
