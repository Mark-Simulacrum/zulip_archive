Topic: Tab-completion of users shouldn't use mouse position

Josh Triplett (Jan 28 2022 at 20:05):

I finally figured out why I often get unexpected results from tab completing a user after typing @.

Josh Triplett (Jan 28 2022 at 20:06):

If you point the mouse at a username, that changes the cursor position, and then if you hit tab, it completes the one the mouse is pointing at.

Josh Triplett (Jan 28 2022 at 20:06):

That's true even if the mouse started out over the menu of possible completions and you didn't move it, or just barely moved it.

Josh Triplett (Jan 28 2022 at 20:08):

I think keyboard-based completion of a username shouldn't take mouse position into account. I don't know if anyone uses the mouse to point and click on a username, but it seems fine for that to work; it just shouldn't affect what you get when you hit tab to complete the name.
